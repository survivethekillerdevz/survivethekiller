## 🔨 Troubleshooting & Known Issues

### ❗ Script Doesn't Execute / GUI Doesn't Appear

**Symptoms:** You paste and execute the script but nothing happens on screen.

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Make sure your executor is properly attached to the Roblox process *before* executing

---

### ❗ ESP Not Rendering / Shows No Players

**Symptoms:** ESP is toggled on but no boxes or tracers appear.

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Increase `ESP_MaxDistance` in config (default is 500, try 1000)
- ✅ Confirm the toggle is on — some executors reset GUI state on re-inject
- ✅ Try rejoining a different server — heavily moderated servers sometimes filter client-side renders
- ✅ Disable conflicting scripts if running multiple GUIs simultaneously

---

### ❗ Double Jump Not Working on Mobile

**Symptoms:** Double jump toggle is on, but the jump doesn't trigger.

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Enable Overlay Permissions for your executor app in Android settings
- ✅ Tap the jump button **twice quickly** — mobile touch debounce can sometimes eat the second input
- ✅ Lower `JumpPower` in config — overly high values can cause physics glitches on mobile

---

### ❗ AutoFarm Stops Mid-Session

**Symptoms:** AutoLoot or AutoRevive stops working after 10–15 minutes.

**Solutions:**
- ✅ Roblox's anti-idle system may kick in — increase player character activity via the `AntiIdle` toggle in Settings
- ✅ Increase `LootDelay` slightly to reduce frequency and lower detection surface
- ✅ Re-execute the script after long idle periods

---

### ❗ Trade Helper Showing Wrong Values

**Symptoms:** Trade value overlay shows outdated or incorrect item valuations.

**Solutions:**
- ✅ The trade data cache refreshes every 6 hours — wait for the next refresh cycle
- ✅ Manually trigger a data sync from the Trade Helper tab → "Refresh Values"
